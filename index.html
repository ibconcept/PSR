PSR: A Practical Model for the Gig Economy

Unlock clarity and efficiency in your freelance projects.

The Challenge of Alignment

The gig economy thrives on flexibility and speed, but often struggles with clear expectations between clients and freelancers. Unclear briefs, shifting feedback, and payment ambiguities can lead to frustration and wasted time. PSR (Problem, Solution, Result) offers a practical solution.

PSR vs. Traditional Frameworks

PSR is designed for the agility of the gig economy, focusing on immediate clarity and outcome-based evaluation.

Framework PSR Difference
Agile Task-based & Immediate vs. Iterative
Waterfall Flexible vs. Rigid
Scrum Ideal for Solo vs. Team-Oriented
